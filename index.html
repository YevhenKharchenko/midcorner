About the Game

RushRail Security Guard places players in charge of maintaining safety within a high-traffic railway station. Responsibilities include inspecting suspicious passengers, checking luggage, monitoring entrances, and responding to escalating security risks. Each level introduces new challenges, increasing complexity and urgency. Your main job? Stop bad stuff from happening while making sure people can still get to their trains on time.

Core Gameplay

  • Monitor passenger movement

  • Inspect bags and verify identities

  • Identify suspicious behavior

  • Intervene in potential threats

  • Maintain order under time pressure
